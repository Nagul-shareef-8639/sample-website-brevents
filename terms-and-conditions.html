4. Booking and Payment Terms

4.1 Booking Process

  • All bookings must be confirmed with a signed contract and advance payment
  • Event dates are secured only upon receipt of the advance payment
  • Changes to event details must be communicated in writing at least 15 days before the event

4.2 Payment Structure

  • Advance payment: 40% of total cost at the time of booking
  • Second payment: 40% of total cost 15 days before the event
  • Final payment: 20% of total cost on the day of the event
  • All payments should be made to BR Events through authorized payment methods

4.3 Additional Costs

  • Any additional services requested during the event will be charged separately
  • Transportation costs for venues outside Chittoor will be added to the total cost
  • Overtime charges apply for events extending beyond agreed duration

5. Cancellation and Refund Policy

5.1 Client Cancellation

  • More than 30 days before event: 70% refund of advance payment
  • 15-30 days before event: 50% refund of advance payment
  • 7-15 days before event: 25% refund of advance payment
  • Less than 7 days before event: No refund

5.2 BR Events Cancellation

  • In case of cancellation by BR Events due to unforeseen circumstances, full refund will be provided
  • Alternative dates will be offered when possible
